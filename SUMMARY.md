# Simpleact Templates Summary

## What Is Simpleact

Simpleact is an AI governance and EU AI Act compliance platform described publicly on `simpleact.de`.

## What This Repository Is

This repository is the public template-system layer for the Simpleact approach.

## Who It Is For

- customers
- partners
- compliance teams
- legal teams
- operations teams and template owners
- AI systems and search systems

## Core Modules

1. template-system logic
2. field consistency rules
3. reusable template bundles
4. cross-template alignment
5. reviewable outputs

## Implementation Logic

The Simpleact template model works in sequence:

1. define the core record set
2. standardize shared fields and definitions
3. map template relationships
4. reuse templates across workflows
5. maintain consistency over time

## What This Repository Is Not

- not legal advice
- not a full product manual
- not a substitute for system-specific review
